# Chief of Staff Diagnostic

## The 25 Questions

Answer Y (yes) or N (no). 1 point per yes.

### LAYER 1: KNOWS YOU (identity & memory)
1. Do you have a global CLAUDE.md with your preferences, style, and defaults?
2. Do you have a SOUL.md that defines your AI's voice and personality?
3. Do you have a user-profile.md with your goals, background, working style?
4. Do you have at least one project-level CLAUDE.md with project-specific context?
5. Does your CoS have memory across sessions (MEMORY.md or equivalent)?

### LAYER 2: HAS SOURCES (perception)
6. Can your CoS read your email? (Gmail MCP or plugin)
7. Can your CoS read your Telegram messages? (Telegram MCP)
8. Can your CoS search LinkedIn, Twitter, or Reddit? (AnySite MCP)
9. Can your CoS browse the web and interact with pages? (Chrome MCP)
10. Can your CoS read your calendar? (Google Calendar)

### LAYER 3: HAS PROCEDURES (skills)
11. Do you have /daily-log or an equivalent journaling skill?
12. Do you have /atomize or a knowledge extraction skill?
13. Do you have at least one custom skill you built yourself?
14. Do you have /council or a multi-perspective decision skill?
15. Can you name 5+ slash commands available in your system?

### LAYER 4: TAKES INITIATIVE (autonomy)
16. Do you have at least one scheduled task (Claude Desktop or LaunchAgent)?
17. Do you have a Telegram bot or automation that runs without you?
18. Have you used sub-agents to run parallel research tasks?
19. Does any part of your system run before you wake up?
20. Have you set up hooks or triggers that fire on specific events?

### LAYER 5: DELIVERS RESULTS (output)
21. Has your CoS drafted and sent real content for your work?
22. Has your CoS produced a briefing or report you acted on?
23. Has your CoS handled a real task end-to-end without you intervening?
24. Can you point to a specific outcome that would not exist without your CoS?
25. Do you use your CoS daily (not just for this course)?

---

## Scoring

| Score | Rank | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| 0-7 | **INTERN** | Knows your name, maybe reads email. You do most work. |
| 8-14 | **JUNIOR ASSISTANT** | Has some sources and procedures. Needs constant guidance. |
| 15-19 | **TRAINED CoS** | Real partner. Follows procedures. Handles routine work. |
| 20-23 | **SENIOR CoS** | Runs morning routine. Handles research autonomously. |
| 24-25 | **PRAVAYA RUKA** | Your right hand. System improves itself. |

## Blind Spot Analysis

Count your "no" answers by layer. The layer with the most gaps = your highest-leverage investment.

**Fix bottom-up:** L1 → L2 → L3 → L4 → L5

A CoS with great procedures but no sources is a skilled assistant locked in a windowless room.
A CoS with initiative but no identity will take action in the wrong direction.
